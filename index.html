Our Mission

The Honey Identity

With over 15 years of experience in honey exporting, what sets SAI GON BEES CO LTD apart is our ability to quickly adhere and comply with new and different standards governed by many countries across the world.

As a Vietnamese raw honey supplier, we specialize in exporting high quality non-adulterated pure honey to different countries in the world. Through a chain of regular visits and careful inspections on our farmers’ honey, we ensure that every last drop of our honey meets the highest international food safety standards.
